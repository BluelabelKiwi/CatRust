About Cats

Cats are one of the most popular pets in the world. They are known for their playful nature and affectionate personalities. Cats are also great companions for people of all ages, and can make great additions to any household.

Fun Cat Facts

  • Cats have been domesticated for over 9,000 years.
  • Cats have a flexible spine and can rotate their ears 180 degrees.
  • Cats spend about 30% of their waking hours grooming themselves.
